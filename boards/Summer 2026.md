# Summer 2026 Anime Guide — The Only List That Matters™

*Last updated: June 18, 2026. Sources: Crunchyroll, HIDIVE, Netflix, MyAnimeList, LiveChart, Anime News Network, GameRant, Anime Corner.*

---

## ISEKAI (Truck-kun's Busiest Season Yet)

- **Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation Season 3**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G24H1N3MP/mushoku-tensei-jobless-reincarnation
    - Rudeus returns to prove that even in your third life, you can still be a massive pervert with a heart of gold.

- **Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- Season 4 (Recapture Arc)**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GRGG9798R/rezero--starting-life-in-another-world-
    - Subaru dies again. And again. And again. At this point death is just his commute.

- **That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Season 4**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GYZJ43JMR/that-time-i-got-reincarnated-as-a-slime
    - Rimuru's nation-building simulator continues, now with 40% more meetings about trade policy.

- **Saga of Tanya the Evil Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GR9P57W96/saga-of-tanya-the-evil
    - A salaryman-turned-loli-war-criminal continues to spite God with extreme prejudice and artillery.

- **Skeleton Knight in Another World Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G8DHV7WJP/skeleton-knight-in-another-world
    - Big skeleton man in armor keeps doing good deeds while everyone screams at his face. Again.

- **Hell Mode: The Hardcore Gamer Dominates in Another World with Garbage Balancing Season 2**
    - https://www.hidive.com/tv/hell-mode
    - The title is longer than most people's attention spans, and the grinding continues.

- **Trapped in a Dating Sim: The World of Otome Games Is Tough for Mobs Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G0XHWM0D3/trapped-in-a-dating-sim-the-world-of-otome-games-is-tough-for-mobs
    - Leon returns to a world where women rule and men are accessories — so basically regular society but with mechs.

- **From Old Country Bumpkin to Master Swordsman Season 2**
    - https://www.amazon.com/Old-Country-Bumpkin-Master-Swordsman/dp/B0D8Z7S2G4 *(Amazon Prime Video exclusive — not on Crunchyroll; premieres July 8)*
    - An old man who trained everyone else's heroes finally gets his own sequel. About damn time.

- **The Exiled Heavy Knight Knows How to Game the System**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378018/the-exiled-heavy-knight-knows-how-to-game-the-system
    - When the meta says heavy knights suck, one man says "watch me" and breaks the entire economy. GoHands animation means it'll either look incredible or give you a seizure — possibly both.

- **The Frontier Lord Begins with Zero Subjects**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378124/the-frontier-lord-begins-with-zero-subjects
    - A war hero retires to manage a territory with literally nothing in it. The ultimate fixer-upper isekai.

- **The Oblivious Saint Can't Contain Her Power (The Unaware Saint Keeps Spreading Her Blessings)**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378125/the-oblivious-saint-cant-contain-her-power
    - A saint who has no idea she's a saint keeps accidentally performing miracles. God's most chaotic employee.

- **From Overshadowed to Overpowered: Second Reincarnation of a Talentless Sage**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378115/from-overshadowed-to-overpowered-second-reincarnation-of-a-talentless-sage
    - Guy gets reincarnated twice and still can't catch a break until he suddenly can. Persistence pays, apparently.

- **Heroine? Saint? No, I'm an All-Works Maid (And Proud of It)!**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378069/heroine-saint-no-im-an-all-works-maid-and-proud-of-it
    - Reincarnated into an otome game and chooses to be the maid instead of the main character. Based and service-industry-pilled.

- **The World's Strongest Rearguard: Labyrinth Country's Novice Seeker**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378122/the-worlds-strongest-rearguard
    - A support-class guy who's secretly OP. It's like being the bass player who actually writes all the songs.

- **Though I Am an Inept Villainess**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/though-i-am-an-inept-villainess *(Crunchyroll, premieres July 12 — page not yet live)*
    - A villainess so bad at being evil she accidentally becomes the heroine. Failing upward: the anime.

- **Hanaori-san Still Wants to Fight in the Next Life**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378066/hanaori-san-still-wants-to-fight-in-the-next-life
    - A delinquent girl reincarnates and immediately looks for someone to throw hands with. Some habits transcend death.

- **Tomb Raider King**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/tomb-raider-king *(Crunchyroll, premieres July 8 — page not yet live)*
    - Mysterious tombs appear worldwide and one guy exploits them better than anyone. Indiana Jones meets gacha mechanics.

- **Ascendance of a Bookworm: Adopted Daughter of an Archduke** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G6793XKZY/ascendance-of-a-bookworm
    - Myne's quest to reinvent the printing press continues. Girl literally would die for a library card.

## FANTASY (Swords, Sorcery, and Questionable Life Choices)

- **Black Torch**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00377907/black-torch
    - A ninja who can talk to animals teams up with a demon cat. Ninja Scroll meets Pet Sematary, but make it shonen.

- **Clevatess: The King of Magical Beasts and the Human Girl Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G8DHV78ZM/clevatess
    - A beast king and a human baby continue their unlikely family dynamic. Parenting is hard, especially when you're a magical lion.

- **Jaadugar: A Witch in Mongolia**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378078/jaadugar-a-witch-in-mongolia
    - A witch in 13th-century Mongolia. Finally, an isekai where the protagonist didn't come from Japan.

- **The Insipid Prince's Furtive Grab for The Throne**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/the-insipid-princes-furtive-grab-for-the-throne *(Crunchyroll, premieres July 6 — page not yet live)*
    - A prince everyone thinks is useless secretly schemes for power. The ultimate "quiet kid in the back of the class" energy.

- **The Fake Alchemist**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/the-fake-alchemist *(streaming TBD — anime just announced June 2026)*
    - An alchemist who can't actually do alchemy fakes it till he makes it. Every STEM major's worst nightmare.

- **Red River**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/red-river *(Crunchyroll, premieres July 7 — page not yet live)*
    - A modern girl gets pulled into ancient Anatolia. Classic shoujo isekai before isekai was even a word.

- **Daemons of the Shadow Realm**
    - https://www.netflix.com/title/82719204
    - Hiromu Arakawa (Fullmetal Alchemist) returns with twins who command supernatural duos. Basically FMA but with sibling issues dialed to 11.

- **The Classroom of a Black Cat and a Witch** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00374357/the-classroom-of-a-black-cat-and-a-witch
    - A witch teaches at a magic school with her cat familiar. Hogwarts but the professor is the main character and the cat has more screen time.

- **Goodbye, Lara (Sayonara Lara)**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378059/goodbye-lara
    - Kinema Citrus's original anime about a mermaid. The Little Mermaid but make it artsy and emotionally devastating.

- **The Ogre's Bride (Oni no Hanayome)**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378121/the-ogres-bride
    - A human girl becomes the bride of an ogre. Beauty and the Beast if the Beast was 8 feet tall and had horns.

- **Kaiju Girl Caramelise**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378081/kaiju-girl-caramelise
    - A girl turns into a kaiju when she gets flustered. Dating is hard enough without accidentally destroying Tokyo.

- **A Livid Lady's Guide to Getting Even: How I Crushed My Homeland with My Mighty Grudges**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00379182/a-livid-ladys-guide-to-getting-even-how-i-crushed-my-homeland-with-my-mighty-grimoires
    - A noble lady gets betrayed and decides revenge is a dish best served with overwhelming military force. Gaslight, gatekeep, girlboss: medieval edition.

## ROMANCE (Love Is in the Air — and Also on Fire, Probably)

- **Smoking Behind the Supermarket with You**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378116/smoking-behind-the-supermarket-with-you
    - A salaryman and a convenience store clerk bond over shared smoke breaks. The most relatable romance of the season for anyone who's ever worked retail.

- **You and I Are Polar Opposites Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00365624/you-and-i-are-polar-opposites
    - The gyaru and the quiet guy continue being adorable together. Opposites attract, and also generate enough electricity to power a small city.

- **Rich Girl Caretaker: I'm Secretly the Caregiver of the Most Popular Girl in This Rich Kid School**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378102/rich-girl-caretaker-im-secretly-the-caregiver-of-the-most-popular-girl-in-this-rich-kid-school
    - A regular guy becomes the secret caretaker of the school's queen bee. The title is a full synopsis, you're welcome.

- **I Want to Love You Till Your Dying Day**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00374354/i-want-to-love-you-till-your-dying-day
    - Yuri dark fantasy at a military academy where girls are trained as weapons. Love and death, together at last. Nothing says romance like "you might explode tomorrow."

- **Love Unseen Beneath the Clear Night Sky**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378087/love-unseen-beneath-the-clear-night-sky
    - A romance where one of them is literally invisible. The ultimate "notice me senpai" scenario.

- **The 100 Girlfriends Who Really, Really, Really, Really, REALLY Love You Season 3**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GNVHKN933/the-100-girlfriends-who-really-really-really-really-really-love-you
    - Rentaro's harem expands further. At this rate he'll hit 100 girlfriends by season 47. Fuck ya.

- **Hanazakari no Kimitachi e (Hana-Kimi) Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00365568/hana-kimi
    - A girl disguises herself as a boy to attend an all-boys school for her idol. Shakespeare walked so Hana-Kimi could run.

- **Kirio Fan Club** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.hidive.com/tv/kirio-fan-club
    - Two high school girls are obsessed with the same guy. A romcom about being a simp, but make it wholesome.

- **Young Ladies Don't Play Fighting Games**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00362362/young-ladies-dont-play-fighting-games
    - Proper young ladies at an elite academy secretly duke it out in fighting games. The FGC has never looked this elegant. Early screening audiences were cracking up the entire time — this might be the sleeper hit of the season.

## ACTION (Punching, Slashing, and General Mayhem)

- **Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War Part 4 — The Calamity**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/G63VGG2NY/bleach
    - The final arc's final part. Ichigo's been fighting this war longer than some fans have been alive.

- **The Elusive Samurai Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GQWH0M19X/the-elusive-samurai
    - A young lord who's really good at running away continues running away. The most historically accurate coward you'll ever root for.

- **The Ghost in the Shell (2026 TV Series)**
    - https://www.amazon.com/THE-GHOST-IN-SHELL/dp/B0H43Y5131 *(Amazon Prime Video exclusive — not on Crunchyroll)*
    - Science SARU reboots the cyberpunk classic. Motoko's back and she's still questioning what it means to be human in a world of AIs. Very topical. July 7.

- **Sekiro: No Defeat** *(movie)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/sekiro-no-defeat *(Crunchyroll after 3-week theatrical run — page not yet live)*
    - FromSoftware's "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice" gets an anime movie. You thought the game was hard? Now you can't even pause it.

- **LIAR GAME** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00371851/liar-game
    - Psychological mind games where deception is the only weapon. Among Us but with actual stakes and better writing.

## DRAMA (Feelings, Feuds, and Existential Crises)

- **The World Is Dancing**
    - https://www.hidive.com/tv/the-world-is-dancing
    - A historical drama about 14th-century Japanese theater. Noh drama about Noh drama — that's meta. Premieres June 29 on HIDIVE.

- **The Drops of God** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00371872/the-drops-of-god
    - A wine-themed drama about inheritance and family. Makes you feel sophisticated just by watching.

- **Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha EXCEEDS Gun Blaze Vengeance**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378090/magical-girl-lyrical-nanoha-exceeds-gun-blaze-vengeance
    - Nanoha returns with more befriending via orbital bombardment. The most violent magical girl franchise is back, and the title now sounds like a heavy metal album.

## SLICE OF LIFE (The Mundane, But Make It Anime)

- **GROW UP SHOW -Sunflower Circus-**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00378063/grow-up-show
    - An original anime about a circus troupe. A-1 Pictures doing something that isn't a sequel — cherish this moment.

- **Chainsmoker Cat (Yani Neko)**
    - https://www.netflix.com/title/82760630 *(Netflix worldwide — not on Crunchyroll)*
    - A degenerate cat girl who smokes. The title tells you everything you need to know about what you're getting into.

- **A Gentle Noble's Vacation Recommendation** *(continuing from Winter)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00365550/a-gentle-nobles-vacation-recommendation
    - A nobleman becomes an adventurer-for-hire on vacation. The ultimate "I'm not working, I'm consulting" energy.

- **Gals Can't Be Kind to Otaku!?** *(continuing from Spring)*
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00371662/gals-cant-be-kind-to-otaku
    - Gyarus and otaku navigate friendship. Two demographics that the internet insists cannot coexist, proving the internet wrong.

## COMEDY (You Will Laugh, This Is a Threat)

- **Grand Blue Dreaming Season 3**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GNVHKN94W/grand-blue-dreaming
    - The diving club that never dives returns. More alcohol poisoning, more naked men, zero actual scuba.

- **Azur Lane: Slow Ahead! Season 2**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GQWH0MXPQ/anime-azurlane-slow-ahead
    - Ship girls doing slice-of-life things. The gacha game spinoff where nobody sinks and everyone just vibes.

- **BanG Dream! Yume∞Mita**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GT00379424/bang-dream-yumemita
    - A new BanG Dream series featuring Mugendai Mewtype. More girls, more bands, more songs you'll hum for weeks.

- **Dodgeball Girl Danko (Honoo no Toukyuujo: Dodge Danko)**
    - https://www.crunchyroll.com/series/GDKHZEJPQ/dodgeball-girl-danko *(ANIPLUS Asia exclusive — not on Crunchyroll; premieres July 6)*
    - A fiery dodgeball anime where the sport looks like actual warfare. If you can dodge a wrench, you can dodge existential dread. Also technically sports, but dodgeball as a serious sport is inherently hilarious.

## SCI-FI (Robots, Cyborgs, and Questionable Futures)

- **Sparks of Tomorrow**
    - https://www.netflix.com/title/81698957
    - Kyoto Animation's steampunk original about bringing electricity to an alternate-history Kyoto. KyoAni doing sci-fi — inject it directly into my veins.

- **Thunder 3**
    - https://www.netflix.com/title/THUNDER3
    - Three middle schoolers get pulled into a parallel world. Netflix's other summer anime that isn't Sparks of Tomorrow.

- **The Ribbon Hero** *(Netflix movie)*
    - https://www.netflix.com/title/THE_RIBBON_HERO
    - A modern reimagining of Tezuka's Princess Knight. Netflix and Twin Engine reviving a classic — actually tasteful for once.
